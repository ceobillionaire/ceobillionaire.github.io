Short version

QUEBEC.AI | Québec Artificial Intelligence is a private Québec corporation founded in 2003, focused on frontier AI, AI‑First enterprise transformation, sovereign AI infrastructure, autonomous agents, security, and strategic AI governance.

Standard version

QUEBEC.AI works at the intersection of frontier artificial intelligence, AI‑First enterprise transformation, sovereign AI infrastructure, autonomous agents, security, assurance, and strategic AI governance. It supports leaders, institutions, builders, and partners strengthening Québec’s capacity to build, govern, deploy, secure, and coordinate artificial intelligence as strategic infrastructure.

Founder & President

Vincent Boucher is President of QUEBEC.AI and MONTREAL.AI. He is a Québec-based AI institution-builder focused on frontier artificial intelligence, AI‑First enterprise transformation, sovereign AI infrastructure, autonomous agents, strategic AI governance, public AI discourse, and the future of machine intelligence as strategic infrastructure.
