Archive

Governed history.

The archive preserves unusual history, older press, technical artifacts, public records, and legacy materials without letting older positioning define the current authority surface.

Current first

Current roles, thesis, correspondence, and institutional routes remain dominant across search, navigation, and page hierarchy.

History labeled

Older materials are dated, contextualized, and marked as archive when they no longer represent the present mandate.

Sources preserved

Press clips, PDFs, videos, repositories, prior-art records, and timeline entries are preserved as provenance.
